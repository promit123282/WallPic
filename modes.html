Manual Mode is a very powerful & complicated mode of camera.It gives you the ability to control evevrythings like aperture, shutter speed, iso, whitebalance etc. But before knowing about mannual mode we have to know about all parameters like aperture, shutter speed ,iso etc.If you know all of these then you can go straight toward Modes.

Modes:-

  1. Full Manual Mode:-

    Inside this mode the camera will give you the ability to control all types of parameters like Shutter-Speed ,Iso ,Aperture etc. But always you have to care about exposure value which is often shown like a slide on the camera screen. When the exposure value becomes zero then your image will be perfectly exposed otherwise it will be under-exposed if the value is lower than zero or it will be over-exposed if the value is greater than zero.So, always try to keep it zero by controlling all of the parameters. You can also make various types of creative images by making it under-exposed or over-exposed.
