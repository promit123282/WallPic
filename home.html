What is Photography?-

"Photography" is an art where the light coming from outside passes through the very small hole of aperture of a camera and it is captured by a "Image Sensor" or by a light-sensitive material such as "Photographic film".Thus the image is captured and it makes a meaningful scene.At the past we have used "Photographic film" but now basically we are using "Image Sensor".From black&white "Photographic Film" we have created images by "Darkroom Process" but now the picture is generated directly and "Digitally".

How to click better Photos?-

  1. Don't move or shake your camera.Try to hold it still.
  2. To click picture at high zoom use tripod.
  3. Always focus on that subject which you want to capture.
  4. Use different camera modes according to the scene like firework, sports, landscape, portrait.
  5. Use different whitebalance according to the light of outside like daylight, flurocent, tungsten.
  6. Use manual mode when the picture quality is more important.
  7. Use other modes when the scene is more important.

Camera Modes:-

Dslr or compact camera have various types of modes to shoot the best pictures.Among these modes the most important and critical mode is Manual mode.Though other modes have various functions,they are not so hard to understand because in these modes camera decides the parameters according to the scene you will set.To read see below.

  • Manual mode:-

    It is a very complicated mode.In this mode your camera gives you the ability to control everything like Aperture.,Shutter speed,Iso etc.If you click picture in this mode ,picture quality will be better.But if you in hurry to capture the scene don't use this mode.
